About

Systems that hold up under pressure

I have led country programmes and financial controls across conflict and displacement settings in Africa and Asia — from Country Director roles to remote financial oversight for large humanitarian operations. That work taught me a simple lesson: the best tools are the ones staff can actually use, offline if needed, without a six-figure licence.

Through Mkweli.tech I design and ship focused products — free sanctions screening for financial teams, farm-to-buyer coordination for African agri systems — and mentor NGOs on practical AI for reporting, compliance, and decision-making.

  • Compliance & integrity
  • Offline-first mobile
  • AI for NGO workflows
