About

Builder first. Operator second.

I am a self-taught AI engineer and product builder based in Grand Baie, Mauritius. I learned by shipping: Android apps, product sites, deploy pipelines, and AI workflows refined with prompt-engineering coursework and daily practice. Inspiration comes from truth-seeking, first-principles builders (including the ethos around systems like Grok) — applied to tools people can actually run.
